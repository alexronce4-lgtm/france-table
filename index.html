Travel Guide
✈️ Your First Trip to France

Planning your first visit? Here are the must-see destinations — from the iconic capital to the glamorous Riviera.

🗼
Paris
Must Visit
The City of Light. See the Eiffel Tower, walk along the Seine, explore the Louvre, and sip café au lait at a sidewalk terrace. Paris is always a good idea.
🌊
Nice / French Riviera
Côte d'Azur
Turquoise Mediterranean waters, the famous Promenade des Anglais, colorful Vieille Ville, and incredible cuisine. The gateway to the French Riviera.
🎬
Cannes
Côte d'Azur
Home of the world-famous Film Festival. Stroll the Boulevard de la Croisette, browse the Marché Forville, and enjoy the glittering coastline.
🏎️
Monaco
Principauté
The tiny principality with big glamour. Visit the Casino de Monte-Carlo, the Prince's Palace, and the Formula 1 circuit winding through the streets.
⛵
Saint-Tropez
Côte d'Azur
The ultimate chic French Riviera village. Sandy beaches, a picturesque harbor packed with yachts, charming cobblestone streets, and legendary summer energy.
🍷
Bordeaux
Wine & Food
The wine capital of the world. Stunning 18th-century architecture, world-class vineyards, and the finest Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot blends.
🍽️
Lyon
Gastronomy
France's culinary capital — home of legendary bouchon restaurants, Paul Bocuse's legacy, and a UNESCO-listed old town full of hidden traboules.
🏰
Strasbourg
Alsace
A fairytale city on the Rhine, blending French elegance and German charm. The stunning Gothic Cathedral, magical Christmas Market, and canals of La Petite France.
🌅
Mont Saint-Michel
Iconic
One of the world's most photographed sites — a medieval abbey perched on a rocky island rising from the sea. Truly magical at high tide, especially at sunset.
