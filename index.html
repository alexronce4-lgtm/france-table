🥐 French Bakeries to Try

Authentic French pastries, croissants, and baguettes just minutes away.

🥖
Petitgrain Boulangerie
A beloved neighborhood boulangerie bringing authentic Parisian bread and pastries to the westside. Known for their hand-crafted sourdough, buttery croissants, and classic pain au chocolat.
View on Google Maps
🧁
The Place To Be
A charming French-inspired café and bakery with a warm, Parisian ambiance. Their freshly baked goods, tartines, and café au lait make it feel like a morning in Paris.
View on Google Maps
🍰
Artelice Pâtisserie
An exquisite French pastry shop showcasing the art of pâtisserie — elegant éclairs, macarons, mille-feuille, and seasonal tarts crafted with classical French technique.

Near Santa Monica
🍷 French Restaurants to Try

Experience the flavors of France right here in Los Angeles.

🥩
Pasjoli
A critically acclaimed French bistro in Santa Monica helmed by Chef Dave Beran. Elevated bistro classics — duck press tableside, steak frites, and refined seasonal menus in a lively, intimate setting.
View on Google Maps
✨
MUSE Santa Monica
A sophisticated French-influenced restaurant offering an elegant dining experience steps from the ocean. Beautiful presentation, curated wine list, and a menu that celebrates the best of French culinary tradition.

Must Visit
